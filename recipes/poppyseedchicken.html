Poppy Seed Chicken

Make this poppy seed chicken recipe tonight!

Need something simple yet tasty? Why not try this delicious chicken recipe? Your family will love it!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb.boneless skinless chicken breast
  • 1 can cream of chicken soup
  • 1 can cream of mushroom soup
  • 1 stick of butter
  • 8 oz of sour cream
  • 2 tbsps of poppy seeds
  • ritz crackers for the topping

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. boil chicken breasts to cook through
  3. shread chicken in bowl
  4. mix in both cans of soup, poppy seeds,and sour cream
  5. spread mixture in 9x13in pan
  6. crush ritz cackers and spread over top
  7. add pats of butter to your liking over the ritz crackers
  8. cook in oven for 25 to 30 min
