Snickerdoodle Cookie Recipe

You will not regret making this homemade, comforting snickerdoodle cookies! This is a great recipe for kids to make!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Cinnamon
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Mix flour, sugar, butter, and cinnamon in a bowl
  3. Fold milk and eggs
  4. Mix all together
  5. Spoon dough into balls and roll in cinnamon sugar mixture
  6. Place on baking sheet and cook for 15 min
