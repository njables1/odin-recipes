Momma Carol's Lasagna

Try this recipie for some delish homemade lasagna!

This lasagna is from an old family recipe passed down from many generations. It is a great, hearty meal to make anytime but especially on a cold winter night.

If you would like to make this recipe, keep scrolling for the list of ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of Ground Beef
  • Half a diced yellow onion
  • Jar of your favorite pasta sauce
  • 1 Box of Lasagna Noodles

Steps

  1. dice onion and saute until soft
  2. add ground beef and cook through
  3. boil water for noodles
  4. add pasta sauce to beef and onion mixture
  5. add noodles to boiling water cook until al dente
  6. layer sauce and noodles in 9 x 13in pan
  7. add mozzarella cheese to top
  8. cook at 375 degrees for 45 min
